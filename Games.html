BATTLE ROYAL

In "Battle Royal," a chapter from Ralph Ellison's Invisible Man, a young Black man is forced to fight other Black boys for the entertainment of white men, symbolizing the brutal racial oppression and dehumanization faced by African Americans in a society that refuses to see their humanity.
